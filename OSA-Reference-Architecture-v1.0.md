```

              Constitutional Space Intelligence System
```

## Constitutional Invariants

1. **All decisions are replayable** — Replay Engine mandatory
2. **All operations produce evidence** — Evidence Ledger mandatory
3. **All policies are compiled, not interpreted** — Policy Engine mandatory
4. **All authority traces to Constitution** — Governance Kernel mandatory
5. **All cross-domain operations federate** — Federation mandatory (EarthOS Pilot B)
6. **All runtimes are verifiable** — Verification Engine mandatory
6. **All data has provenance** — Knowledge Graph + Evidence Ledger mandatory
